# Acme Corp Shipping Policy

**Last Updated:** December 15, 2023

## 1. Shipment Processing Time
All orders are processed within **2-3 business days**. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.
If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in shipment of your order, we will contact you via email or telephone.

## 2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates
Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

| Shipment Method | Estimated Delivery Time | Shipment Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Standard Shipping | 3-5 business days | Free |
| Expedited Shipping | 2 business days | $12.95 |
| Overnight Delivery | 1-2 business days | $24.95 |

* Delivery delays can occasionally occur.

## 3. Shipment to P.O. Boxes or APO/FPO Addresses
Acme Corp ships to addresses within the U.S., U.S. Territories, and APO/FPO/DPO addresses.

## 4. International Shipping
We currently do not ship outside the United States.

## 5. Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking
You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.

## 6. Damages
Acme Corp is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.
